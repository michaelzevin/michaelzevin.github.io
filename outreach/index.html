Astronomy on Tap

Astronomy on Tap is a worldwide series of informal presentations on astronomy, given at local bars/breweries. Along with several other grad students and postdocs, I helped start the local Chicago chapter (Facebook, Twitter). We've been fortunate to partner with some pretty great Chicago area breweries, such as Smylie Bros Brewing, Half Acre Beer Company, and Begyle Brewing so far.
